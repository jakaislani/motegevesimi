Who Is Kweisi Mfume, Tiffany McMillan’s Husband

Kweisi Mfume (born Frizzell Gerald Gray) is an American politician and the former President/CEO of the National Association for the Advancement of Colored People (NAACP), as well as a five-term Democratic Congressman from Maryland’s 7th congressional district, serving in the 100th through 104th Congress.

On September 12, 2006, he lost a primary campaign for the United States Senate seat that was being vacated by Maryland U.S. Senator Paul Sarbanes. On February 4, 2020, Mfume won the Democratic nomination for his former seat in a special election in MD-07 and won the special election on April 28, 2020. He easily got a landslide win, defeating Republican opponent, Kimberly Klacik in the general election on April 28, 2020. He is also vying in the overlapping regular 2020 election for the same seat.
