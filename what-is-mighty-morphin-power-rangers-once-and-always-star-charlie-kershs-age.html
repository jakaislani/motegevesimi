What is Mighty Morphin Power Rangers: Once and Always star Charlie Kershs age?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 24, 2024

The original cast of Mighty Morphin Power Rangers has been reunited in Netflix’s hour-long special episode Once and Always, or, at least part of it. Sadly, Thuy Trang’s absence was particularly felt during the episode, but Netflix found a nice way to honor her legacy as Trini Kwan, by introducing the character’s teen daughter to the franchise — Minh Kwan, played by Charlie Kersh.

Recommended Videos

Minh Kwan’s arc was heavily connected to her late Ranger mother and, despite looking quite young, Kersh did quite a good job on the episode, and is a welcomed addition to the Power Rangers universe. Her martial arts training was put to good use in this new professional adventure, something that not all teenagers could pull off so easily. Perhaps because of it, fans who already had the time to set their sight on this nostalgia-inducing installment and currently searching for all the information they can find on the actress. Questions about her age and birthday, however, definitely stand out among viewers’ wonderings.

How old is Charlie Kersh?

While there’s no public information about the actress’ exact birth date, we know that Charlie Kersh was born in 2006, and is currently 16 years old. She has been in the industry since she was a child, having signed with an agency at five years old, and started off her career by appearing in commercials. Acting is not Kersh’s only talent, though. At age nine, her golden pipes landed her a Broadway role, and ever since, she has been steadily paving her own road to success.
