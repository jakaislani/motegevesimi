8 Things You Didnt Know About Sharry Mann

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 14, 2024

➡ Sharry Mann Biography

Surinder Singh Mann, popularly known as Sharry Mann, is known for being so talented, famed Indian Punjabi playback singer and working in the Indian Punjabi music industry. He is a person who is blessed with multiple skills that make him set apart from others. His one of the top-ranked, latest released songs may include ‘Gumaan’ for which he got much acclaim and immense love from his fans and music lovers. It is said that he took the start of his career in 2011 with the song named ‘Yaar Anmulle’. Later, he made his acting debut in 2013 through a film named ‘Oye Hoye Pyaar Ho Gaya’ for which he got a massive response from the critics. Well, his success is long-lasting so there is much more to know. Let us have a look!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Sharry Mann

1- Multi-talented Star

Surinder Singh Mann aka Sharry Mann is an Indian Punjabi playback singer. But his skills are not limited to just singing, yet he is a great lyricist, film actor, and vocalist as well.

2- Biggest Achievement

Among many notable achievements, the playback singer, Sharry Mann was honored with the Best Music Video Award at brit Asia TV Music Awards for the song titled ‘Yaar Chadeya’ in the year 2019.

3- Holds a Degree in Civil Engineering

Surinder Singh Mann studied at the Rode Lande College, Moga, Punjab, India where he pursued a degree in the field of Civil Engineering. But he had no interest to proceed with it as a career and developed an interest in acting and singing.

4- Avid Reader

Surinder Singh Mann is an avid reader as he has a habit of reading since his childhood. Following reading habits, he still loves reading and manages time to for reading books.

5- Music – first love

Being a Punjabi music sensation, the actor loves his profession and he calls it his first love of life. But when it comes to his favorite singers, he never stops praising his all-time favorite singers including Gurdas Mann, Armaan Malik, Abhijeet Sawant, and Alka Yagnik.

6- Favorite Actors

His favorite actors are Babbu Mann (his brother), Aamir Khan, Salman Khan, and Preity Zinta as they are popular for their wonderful performances and dedication to their profession.

7- Other Favorites

His favorite holiday destination is Melbourne. His favorite accessory is Goggles and one of his favorite goggle brands is Rayban. His favorite colors are Black and Blue. His favorite comedian is Bhagwant Mann.

8- Favorite Movies

Being a movie lover, the actor loves watching movies whenever he gets time, but when it comes to his favorites, he counts a few names including ‘Bollywood film named ‘PK’ starring Aamir Khan, and another one is ‘Angrej’.
