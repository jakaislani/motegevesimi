Jeong Yun-ho Facts

  • He debuted with the boy band, ATEEZ, on October 24, 2018, with the debut extended play, Treasure EP.1: All to Zero, which peaked at number #7 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart and at number #12 on the US World Albums (Billboard) chart.
  • On October 8, 2019, Jeong Yun-ho released his first studio album, Treasure EP.Fin: All to Action, with ATEEZ and the album comprised of songs such as End of the Beginning, If Without You, Win, Sunrise, and Beginning of the End. Also, it was ranked at number #1 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart, at number #7 on US World Albums (Billboard) chart, and at number #10 on US Heatseekers Albums (Billboard) chart.
  • He has also appeared on a number of reality shows like Code Name Is Ateez, Ateez Long Journey, and Ateez Treasure Film.
  • With ATEEZ, he has been the recipient of several accolades like the Soribada Best K-Music Award, MTV EMA Award, and Golden Disc Award.
  • Jeong Yun-ho has released various albums, tracks, and EPs with ATEEZ and some of them are Treasure EP.2: Zero to One, Treasure Epilogue: Action to Answer, Say My Name, and Illusion.
  • Just like Choi San, he also auditioned for the JTBC survival reality show, Mix Nine, and could not qualify.

  • He is right-handed.
  • He is a fan of the Harry Potter movies and also has a wand that he bought in Japan.

  • One of his secret talents is his ability to juggle.
