Katie Holmes Dating New Boyfriend Emilio Vitolo Jr.

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Katie Holmes, the 41 years old American actress, has found her love. She is dating a handsome hunk named Emilio Vitolo Jr., who is a restaurateur. The lovebirds were spotted dining at Peasant Restaurant in Manhattan, where Holmes appeared to be sitting on the chef's lap.

In the pictures acquired by DailyMail, the lovebirds can be seen sharing a passionate kiss and enjoying their time together at Peasant Restaurant.

Holmes, who was previously married to actor Tom Cruise, donned in a plain white tee with a casual top with a pair of denim jeans and had her hair tied up into a messy bun. She was makeup-free during the meetup.

Similarly, Emilio accompanied her with a matching white T-shirt and a cap.

Emilio Vitolo Jr's Net Worth

Emilio Vitolo Jr. makes his net worth through his professional career as a chef.

Vitolo is behind Nolita restaurant Emilio's Ballato, and he is often seen working with his brother Anthony. He owns Emilio's Ballato in SoHo since the early 90s, founded by his father, Emilio Vitolo Sr. Despite his business, he has managed to conceal his net worth details and income.

Meanwhile, his girlfriend, Katie Holmes, has a net worth of $25 million through her acting career. She is known for her works in The Ice Storm (1997), Phone Booth (2002), Batman Begins (2005), Don't Be Afraid of the Dark (2010), Touched with Fire (2015), Ocean's 8 (2018), The Secret: Dare to Dream (2020), and more.

Who Is Emilio Vitolo Jr.?

Emilio Vitolo Jr. has made his prominence in the eatery business. He is a chef working in his family restaurant Emilio's Ballato.

Besides that, Vitolo is an actor and a model as well. He has appeared in the six-episode arc of Royal Pains, has a guest role on Inside Amy Schumer, and in the 2019 indie film Tapestry.

Also, Vitolo is acquainted with many celebs, including Joe Jonas and his wife, Sophie Turner, Nick Jonas, Bella Hadid, and many more.
