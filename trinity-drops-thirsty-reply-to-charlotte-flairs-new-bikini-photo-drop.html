Trinity Drops Thirsty Reply To Charlotte Flairs New Bikini Photo Drop

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Naomi might have walked out of WWE last May, but she still follows a lot of her former co-workers on social media, and that includes Charlotte Flair. A recent post from The Queen garnered an unexpected reaction.

Naomi, who is now going as Trinity on her own, saw that Charlotte Flair posted a bikini photo on X, formerly known as Twitter. That black bikini photo drew a lot of responded from Charlotte’s fans, including Trinity.

The former WWE Women’s Champion posted to respond after Charlotte Flair’s smoking photo drop. Flair only included a few emojis as her caption, but Trinity’s response said more than enough.

Groceries! Instacart me pls

Trinity’s response alludes to something that she would like to eat, and since that photo was of Charlotte Flair’s backside, then it wasn’t hard to connect the dots. We’ll have to see if there are any responses.

Charlotte Flair might not want to publicly reply to this one, because a DM might be the better setting for a response to something like that. If anything, fans certainly got a kick out of things.
