How much is Tom Bradys net worth in 2023

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, May 2, 2024

Tom Brady is an American retired professional football quarterback, spokesman, and entrepreneur with a net worth of US$300 million. With a twenty-two year successful career in the National Football League (NFL) and one of the highest players in the league, there is no doubt how he made his fortune.

Brady may have earned primarily from his lucrative contracts and endorsement deals signed. That been said, the legend also makes huge sums of money every year from his numerous business ventures and investments.

Who is Tom Brady?

Thomas Edward Patrick Brady Jr., otherwise known as Tom Brady is a retired American football quarterback who was born on August 3, 1977 in San Mateo, California, United States. He is the only male child among the four children birthed by Galynn Johnson and Thomas Brady Sr. Brady Jr. is an American with Irish descent, while his mother has Swedish, Norwegian, German, and Polish ancestry.

Tom Brady spent his first 20 seasons with the New England Patriots and was a central contributor to the franchise’s dynasty from 2001 to 2019. In his final three seasons, he was a member of the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Brady is widely regarded as the greatest quarterback of all time

How many houses and cars does Tom Brady have?

Tom Brady and his wife Gisele bought a US$17 million mansion in Los Angeles. The couple own other houses and properties in Miami, Tampa Bay, Brookline, and Tribeca.

Reports have it that Tom Brady owns more than twelve (12) luxury cars in his garage including Bugatti Veyron, Rolls Royce Ghost, Aston Martin DB11, Ferrari M458, Aston Martin DB11, Audi R8, Range Rover, Cadillac Escalade, Lexus GS, BMW X5, Audi S5, Lexus RX and more.

How much does Tom Brady make per year?

According to Pro Football Network, Tom Brady was earning a whooping US$15 million and US$10 million roster bonuses per year from his final contract with the Buccaneers.

What investments does Tom Brady have?

Tom Brady is a renowned investor. All through his career to his retirement in 2022, the former American professional football has made numerous investments in his own businesses and other business ventures all over.

How many Endorsement deals does Tom Brady have?

During Tom Brady’s playing time, he was the go-to guy for brands and companies. He penned numerous deals with companies such as Under Armour, IWC watches, Molecular Mattresses, Aston Martin, Upper Deck, Ugg, Footlocker, Tag Heuer, Sam Adams, FTX & Hertz among others. It has been reported that those endorsement deals made him a total of US$100 million in earnings.

How many Philanthropy works has Tom Brady supported?

Tom Brady is a known philanthropist who has stretched his hands in support of several foundations and non-profit organizations with causes such as cancer, education, poverty and sports etc.

Some of the charitable organizations he has worked with include Make-A=Wish Foundation, Best Buddies International, KaBOOM!, Boys & Girls Clubs of America, PeyBack Foundation and Entertainment Industry Foundation among others.
