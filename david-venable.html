David Venable Biography

David Venable was born on November 12, 1964, in Charlotte, North Carolina, United States of America. David graduated from the University of North Carolina before pursuing a career in food production. He also finished his bachelor’s degree in television, film, and radio.

David Venable Age, Height, Weight

David Venable was born on November 12, 1964, and is 57 years old as of 2023. His height is 1.98 meters and his weight is 86 kilograms.

Career

He did not have to wait long after finishing his studies to begin his career because he received numerous offers and was hired by numerous US television stations. He was a reporter for WOAY-TV in West Virginia and WTAJ-TV in Pennsylvania at the time. After a while, he launched his own TV show, Action Newsmakers.

The show’s theme was public affairs. It was a big hit, and it helped him become very popular among the general public. When he hosted the Children’s Miracle Network telethon, his popularity skyrocketed. In the year 1993, David was hired by QVC. His role at the company was to promote gourmet food products. He gradually rose through the ranks of the channel, eventually hosting a few of his own shows there.

Kitchen With David was his most famous show, and it made David a household name. There are two episodes per week on the show. David was seen cooking and serving some of his recipes on the show. His recipes were fantastic, and he quickly became well-known. David published his first cookbook in 2012. There are over 150 recipes in the book. Food lovers from all over the world praised the book.

David Venable Achievements and Awards

David has yet to win a major award. But we all know that he has made a significant contribution to cooking shows on television for over 24 years, and many newspapers have praised him for it. The ‘New York Times even went so far as to label him “The Man Who Helps America Cook.”
